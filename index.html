Core Features

Everything the placement cell needs, in one place

One platform for student profiles, AI-ranked recommendations and admin-side allocation tracking.

🧠

AI-Powered Matching

Every internship is scored against a student's skills, CGPA, domain preference and certifications — out of 100, with the breakdown shown.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Students see their own recommendations and applications. Admins get a separate panel for managing students, internships and allocations.

👤

Full Student Profiles

Add, edit and update profiles with skills, soft skills, certifications, projects and a resume — full CRUD from the management screen.

📊

Admin Analytics

Department-wise breakdowns, allocation status and downloadable CSV reports, all from a single dashboard.

🎯

Ranked Recommendations

Internships are sorted by match percentage first, so the most relevant opportunity is always at the top of the list.

📋

Application Tracking

Apply with a cover letter and follow the status from Pending through Shortlisted, Accepted or Rejected.
