Core Features

Everything You Need

A complete platform for AI-powered internship matching, student management and career guidance.

🧠
AI-Powered Matching

Sophisticated algorithm analyzes skills, CGPA, interests and certifications to compute a precise match score for every internship.

🔐
Role-Based Login

Separate dashboards for Students and Admins. Students see their AI recommendations; Admins manage everything from one panel.

👤
Student Profiles

Full CRUD interface to add, edit, update and delete student profiles with skills, projects, certifications and more.

📊
Admin Analytics

Admin panel with live charts, allocation tracking, department-wise analytics and downloadable CSV reports.

🎯
Smart Recommendations

Personalized internship recommendations ranked by match percentage to help students find the perfect opportunity.

📋
Application Tracking

Track applications from submission to allocation with status updates: Pending, Shortlisted, Accepted or Rejected.

How It Works

Apply directly with a cover letter and track your application status in real time from your dashboard.
